Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered Italian dish, typically consisting of wide, flat pasta noodles interspersed with sauces and various ingredients such as cheese, meat, and vegetables. The most common version features layers of pasta, tomato sauce, and a combination of cheeses like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. The dish is assembled in a baking pan and then cooked in the oven, resulting in a hearty, flavorful casserole. Lasagna is beloved for its rich, comforting flavors and is often served as a main course.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
